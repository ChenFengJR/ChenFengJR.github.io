About me

I am the product manager of intelligent sorting products in HGTECH. I received my Ph.D. degree from the School of Artificial Intelligence and Automation at Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) in 2024, advised by Prof. Zhiguo Cao and Prof. Yang Xiao. During my doctoral studies, my research focused on computer vision, deep learning, few-shot learning, and object detection/recognition.

Currently, my primary work and research interests lie in intelligent manufacturing, flexible cross-scenario object grasping and handling, multimodal large model integration, and industrial embodied AI. I am dedicated to deeply integrating cutting-edge algorithmic research with practical industrial application scenarios, promoting the deployment and performance breakthroughs of standardized industrial products, and building omni-functional sorting & handling robot.
